T-shirt printing and design

Information learnt

Types of printing:

1. Screen printing (silkscreen printing)
2. Heat transfer printing (laser/ inkjet)
3. Vinyl heat transfer
4. Stenciling
5. Embroidery
6. Dye sublimation

Types of shirt materials:

1. Cotton
2. Synthetic
3. Polyester (drifit)
4. Polyester-cotton

Design and text rule

Pocket logo: 3.5 inch to 5.5 inch
Centered design placement (front): 3.5 inch down from the collar
(design size: 11 inch by 11 inch)

Machine used for T-shirt printing

When printing, ensure to flatten out T-shirt to prevent wrinkles.
For Dye Sublimation, one requirement is to wait for around 40-50 seconds before releasing the cover.
After printing, immediately take off printing paper
