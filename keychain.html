Personalized keychain

For this keychain, I used Autodesk Fushion 360

Tools used for formation of Keychain:

1. 'Create sketch' command under Solid

I ensure that my parameters are according to this:

2. 'Press Pull'

I used this command to form the shape into a 3D figure. Ensuring the thickness to be 3mm as stated in the parameter.

3. 'Fillet'

This command was used to "file" down the edges of the keychain and gives it a rounded look.
This also disables risk of accidentally harming oneself when carrying the keychain around.

4. 'Text'

As seen in the picture, my name is located in the middle of the keychain. This was only possible with the help of the 'text' command that enables me to insert "TMeiFang" inside.
